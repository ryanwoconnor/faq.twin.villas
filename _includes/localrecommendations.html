Local Recommendations

Beaches

  • duBois Beach
    • Address: 1 Water St, Stonington, CT 06378
    • Other Information: This is definitely the closest beach to the house. To sit at the beach you will need to purchase a day pass. There is also a big parking lot here with a grassy area with picnic tables that does not require a beach pass. There are also amazing views where you can see New York and Rhode Island if you look out across the water.
  • Misquamicut State Beach
    • Address: 257 Atlantic Avenue, Westerly, RI 02891
    • Other Information: Be aware that this is a popular beach. So if it is the summertime, you'll want to leave quite early to get there. You should also check the website for specific parking information including cost.
  • East Matunuck State Beach
    • Address: 950 Succotash RdSouth Kingstown, RI 02879
    • Other Information: This is a popular surf spot.
