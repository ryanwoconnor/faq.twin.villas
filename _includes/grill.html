Grill

The grill outside is available for use. Please also abide by the following rules:

  • The Grill is a shared ammenity.
  • Grill should be monitored and attended at all times. Do not leave grill unattended for any reason. For example: Do not attempt to cook ribs and leave the grill on for several hours and leave the house.
  • After use, please ensure the propane is completely shut off using the knobs, as well as on the tank itself.
  • Please ensure all utensils are removed from the grill, and brought back to the listing once you are done. This includes any forks, knives, spatulas, etc.
  • Do not leave any food on the grill after you're done using it.
  • Please cover the grill only once it has cooled down.
