Safety Lights

As a safety mechanism, we have several lights throughout the Airbnb. These lights are constantly charged via the power outlet they are plugged into. In the event of a power outage they can be unplugged and will double as a flashlight.

When these are plugged in, the lights will only automatically turn on when it is dark. The light sensor is located as shown below.

If you would like to turn the light off, press the power button on the side of the light.

Note that some guests may power these off using the button, however they still will be charging.
