Lost and Found

If you accidentally leave something behind at one of our listings, have no fear! If we find it, we will be happy to ship it back to you. Whether it is a phone charger, smart watch charger, or even a coat - we've got your back.

We only require:

  • That you pay for the shipping costs prior to us mailing the item back.
  • That you understand this is a best-effort service. So we cannot guarantee same day shipping.
  • That you provide us with a valid address to ship the item back to.
As far as payment details for shipping:
  • If you've booked through Airbnb, we will request the money through the Airbnb resolution center
  • If you've booked directly with us, we will ask that you send us money through Venmo, Zelle, or some other method
