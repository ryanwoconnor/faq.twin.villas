Toilet Paper and Paper Towels

We provide one roll of toilet paper (per bathroom) and one roll of paper towels (per kitchen) for guests. So for our properties that have two bathrooms, you'll find a roll of toilet paper in each.

These amenities are "what you see is what you get". We cannot provide additional paper towels or toilet paper. So please remember extra if you're staying for a period of time where you think you'll need additional.
